What is FarmTracker?

Farm, Barn, Field Tracking Application

This application allows you to track your plants and animals easily and precisely. It provides easy recording of information such as spraying, fertilization, irrigation, product collected, product sold, age of your plants and trees. Likewise, it allows you to track various information of your animals such as vaccine, age and weight.
